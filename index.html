I stopped eating at restaurants. I realized that I really don’t know what is actually being put in what I am eating. I can tell the restaurant to make it a certain way but it cost extra money and sometimes I’m not sure if they did it or not. If I do get a hankering for pizza or fries, I make it myself at home and cut the portions in half.



I like cooking my own food.
