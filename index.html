lifestyle

I don’t want to rely on a gym membership to workout.




While working out at gyms during peak times like the morning or after 5:00 PM, I don’t like what I have to put up with. I cannot workout late evenings when the gym is scarce because I won’t be able to sleep afterwards. I dislike it while I’m working out at the gym and someone is waiting for the machine that I am currently using. I feel rushed and irritated. When I workout I like having my own space and freedom. Also parking at gyms are extremely limited and that just ruins my workout from the get.


My point is I found too many things I didn’t like when I had a gym membership. Those things that I didn’t like gave me more excuses to skip out on my workouts. I didn’t feel motivated and I didn’t want to spend more money on a personal trainer on top of the monthly gym memberships. When I discovered Fitbit back in 2010, I stopped the gym membership and decided to give Fitbit a chance.

I want to be healthy and give myself a chance to live longer.




They say that you cannot entirely move forward until you stop looking back at your past.


It sounds cliché but that is how I truly feel about my life. For the longest time I didn’t know where to begin. I always found excuses to not workout or eat healthy foods. However, I had to face myself and realize that I won’t live long if I keep eating unhealthy and not working out.

I started to eat healthy and workout. I took small steps. It wasn’t as bad as I thought it would be. I don't have a support system and I had people in my life at a certain time that made me feel bad about the way I looked. I cut those people out of my life because they didn’t do anything but make me feel bad for trying to better myself. It was the best decision I made and the most important is that I did these things for myself and not for anyone else.

When I got my first Fitbit back in 2010, it worked until I accidentally put it in the washer. After that I didn’t have a Fitbit for awhile. In 2014 I bought my second Fitbit. I started with walking because I have bad knees. Then I decided to add light jogging and increased it to running in place. I haven’t run on concrete yet because of my knees. Right now, I’m running in place on the grass and its quite a workout itself that is a challenge for me. I plan to buy the Fitbit that counts my heart rate soon.

foods



I like eating colorful foods.



I like all the colors that comes with different foods. However, I think that fruits and vegetables are so colorful and delicious to eat. For some reason, foods that are colorful taste better to me. I also like the freshness of fruits and vegetables. Maybe that’s how I associate colorful foods with freshness.

I stopped eating at restaurants. I realized that I really don’t know what is actually being put in what I am eating. I can tell the restaurant to make it a certain way but it cost extra money and sometimes I’m not sure if they did it or not. If I do get a hankering for pizza or fries, I make it myself at home and cut the portions in half.



I like cooking my own food and substituting recipes.


I’ve always like to cook. Its just that it is time consuming but its not as expensive as eating at a restaurant. The good thing is I know what’s going in my food and I’m able to use fresh quality ingredients. It does take a lot of patience to cook food. I usually like making recipes that can slow-cook all day on the stove or in the oven. Or sometimes I just like having a salad with grilled chicken and fruit. That’s usually when I don’t have time to cook. I also keep a lot of eggs in my refrigerator and those are my usual protein after working out.



Many recipes like pizza and hamburgers can be substituted with other ingredients. What I learned from cooking is that certain ingredients should be in moderation and portions should be cut in half. I use a lot of spices in my cooking which gives it a lot of flavor. One time I made burgers out of lentils and carrots. It was one of the best burgers I have ever had! There’s substitute for just about every recipe!

workouts

There are so many workouts that you can do with Fitbit!


Fitbit will record every single activity that you do. You just need to sync your Fitbit to your smartphone or computer. You are not limited to just walking and running. I like that Fitbit records my activity time, steps taken, distance, and the calories burned. I can also go back on different dates of the calendar to see how far I’ve come and set up more challenges. I like to get my steps by running in place on grass.
